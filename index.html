About the Company

Holding value.
Building growth.

CNSX Global Markets Inc. is the parent holding company for a growing international portfolio of exchange and market infrastructure assets. Incorporated in Canada, the Group was established to consolidate and expand a suite of exchange holdings under unified strategic ownership.


The Group owns the Canadian Securities Exchange (CSE), one of the world's fastest-growing public equity exchanges, and the National Stock Exchange of Australia (NSX), as well as other innovative financial services companies.


The Group's leadership team includes executives drawn from across its exchange network, with deep expertise in capital markets regulation, finance, and operations.
