About the Company

Built to hold.
Built to grow.

CNSX Global Markets Inc. is the parent holding company for a growing international portfolio of exchange and market infrastructure assets. Incorporated in Canada, the Group was established to consolidate and expand a suite of exchange holdings under unified strategic ownership.


The Group's flagship holding is the Canadian Securities Exchange (CSE), one of the world's fastest-growing public equity exchanges. It also holds a majority interest in the National Stock Exchange of Australia (NSX), as well as strategic investments in financial technology through Tetra Digital.


Legal structuring for the Group was led by Tracy Choma Hugo, and the board includes leadership drawn from across the Group's exchange network.

Leadership

The people behind the Group.

RC
Richard Carleton
Chief Executive Officer

Richard Carleton was appointed CEO of the Canadian Securities Exchange in July 2011. Under his leadership, the CSE has set consecutive records for new listings, capital raised, and trading turnover since 2014. An early advocate for the cannabis industry, Richard is recognized by the Financial Post as one of Canada's "25 Cannabis Industry Power Players." He is a member of the Law Society of Ontario since 1987, holds a B.A. from the University of Ottawa and an LL.B. from the University of Toronto, and has completed the executive development programme at the Wharton School.

TS
Tracey Stern
Chief Legal Officer, General Counsel & Corporate Secretary

Tracey Stern and her team are responsible for risk, legal and compliance matters at the CSE. Tracey joined after more than two decades at the Ontario Securities Commission, where she led market structure policy and the regulation of exchanges and alternative trading systems. She served as Chair of IOSCO Committee 2 on the Regulation of Secondary Markets, spanning 30+ jurisdictions, and is co-author of A Framework for Responsive Market Regulation (Oxford University Press, 2019). She holds a B.A. from Western University and a J.D. from Osgoode Hall Law School.

MAP
Mary Anne Palangio
Chief Financial Officer

Mary Anne Palangio is CFO of the Canadian Securities Exchange, responsible for financial planning and reporting, administration, HR, and working closely with the CEO on strategy and development. She was previously CFO at Converge Technology Solutions and LOGiQ Asset Management, and spent over seven years at Manulife in senior operations and technology roles. Mary Anne started her career at Deloitte and holds a CPA, CA and CFA designation, and a B.A. in Commerce and Economics from the University of Toronto.
